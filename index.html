What I Do

I am a talented and results-oriented creative professional who brings brands to life.

Creative Direction

Experience managing creative teams, agencies, contractors, and vendors for B2B and B2C marketing efforts for global brands, startups, and mid-size businesses.

Digital Ads & Design

Experience producing and designing diverse content for digital and print ads, paid social ads, landing pages, custom email campaigns, e-commerce stores, and responsive websites.

Print Ads & Design

Experience bringing the brand voice to life while delivering results with print ads, product cards, brochures, corporate collateral, packaging, and direct mail campaigns.

Video & Motion Graphics

Experience producing motion graphics and video. Adding motion graphics is a great way to increase engagement, drive conversions, and bring a story to life.

Photo Shoot Direction

Experience producing shoots, managing production, budgets, concepts, storyboards, shot lists, hair, makeup, wardrobe, props, sets, talent, permits, release forms, music rights, and editing.

Branding & Rebranding

Experience with brand building for companies and new products in various markets. Your brand is your organization’s reputation, visibility in the marketplace, core values, and beliefs.
