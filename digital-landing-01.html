Destination Landing Page Campaign

B2C Landing Pages — Built, Launched and Measured using Unbounce

the ask

Set Jet, based in Scottsdale, is a membership-based private jet charter program that provides luxurious jet travel at an affordable price. The company requested dedicated landing pages for various email and social campaigns to help drive traffic to their membership signup form.

the answer

Staying true to Set Jet’s brand standards, I designed campaign-specific landing pages with a single call to action and no website navigation. These landing pages helped drive awareness, new memberships, and revenue; while keeping brand awareness top-of-mind.
